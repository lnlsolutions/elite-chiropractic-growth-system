After A Car Accident

Why You Shouldn't Ignore Symptoms

Many accident injuries are not immediately obvious. What feels like minor soreness can become more significant if left untreated. Seeking evaluation after an accident can help identify issues early.

Whiplash

A sudden back-and-forth movement of the neck during impact that commonly causes pain, stiffness, headaches, and reduced mobility.

Neck Pain

Neck discomfort is one of the most common symptoms reported after motor vehicle accidents.

Back Pain

Impact-related stress on the spine can lead to ongoing discomfort and limitations.

Headaches

Many accident victims experience headaches related to neck injuries and muscle tension.

Common Warning Signs

Symptoms That May Appear Days Later

  • Neck stiffness
  • Lower back pain
  • Headaches
  • Shoulder discomfort
  • Numbness or tingling
  • Difficulty sleeping
  • Reduced range of motion
  • Pain while working or driving
